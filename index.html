monday.com Snooze App - How to Use

Step 1: Install the App

Click on the link below to install the Snooze App.

Install the Snooze App

Step 2: Authenticate the App

Follow the authentication process to allow the app to access your data securely.

Authenticate the App

Step 3: Using the App

Click on the three dots next to an item and select 'Snooze'. Choose the time to snooze the item for and click 'Snooze'.

Step 4: Team Visibility and Notification

Once an item is snoozed, it will be marked accordingly, and your team can see that it has been snoozed. This helps manage expectations and keeps everyone informed about task statuses without manual updates.

When the snooze period expires, the item returns to its original state and a notification is sent to remind you to address the item. This ensures that nothing slips through the cracks.
